I am a theoretical linguist currently working as a post-doc at the Department of English and American Studies at the University of Vienna. My research explores the intersection of pragmatics and prosody, that is how meaning is shaped by intonation. For my PhD (supervised by Prof. Evelien Keizer), I looked into the prosody of information packaging (think, among other notions, Topic, Focus, Contrast) in spontaneously produced British English. Now, in my post-doc project, I am investigating the discourse-structural features of Contrast in more detail, focussing on its grammatical (i.e. morphosyntactic and prosodic) marking in contemporary English.

I approach my research through the lense Functional Discourse Grammar. You can learn more about the theory, its basic architecture, as well as the researchers active in the field [here](http://www.functionaldiscoursegrammar.org/). Since 2021, I am also a member of the Fun\*Cog research group. More information about who we are and what we do can be found [here](https://anglistik.univie.ac.at/staff/teams-and-research-areas/funcog/).
